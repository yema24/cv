Details Of Me

I like to get up late; I like to be alone, I like to be surrounded by people.

I like country’s peace, I like metropolis’ noise; I like the beautiful west

lake in Hangzhou, I like the flat cornfield in Champaign. I like delicious food and comfortable shoes;

I like good books and romantic movies. I like the land and the nature, I like people. And, I like to laugh.
